Instruction


1)There are 3-Level in this game and 3 lifelines in level 2and 3, also at each level you have to take 90 out of 100 points to complete the level and also to get a surprise gift.

2) In the 1st level, you have to clean your House in this level you will be having a broom and when you will complete the 1st level you will get the smaller Sweeping Machine.

3) In the 2nd level, you have to clean your Surrounding area and when you will complete the 2nd level you will get the bigger Sweeping Machine.


4) In the 3rd level, you have to clean your City and when you will complete the 3rdlevel you will win the Swachh Barath trophy and the game will end.


5) To jump press the spacebar and to move at the side right and left arrow key button.
